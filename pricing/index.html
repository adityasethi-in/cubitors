Basic scope rule

Basic pricing covers limited, well-defined work. Larger scopes, urgent delivery, complex integrations, advanced dashboards, AI usage, compliance, and ongoing support are quoted separately.

  • Final amount is confirmed in writing before work starts.
  • Paid tools, APIs, SMS, email, maps, hosting, server, and domain charges are separate.
  • Maintenance, changes after approval, and new features are separate unless included in the proposal.
